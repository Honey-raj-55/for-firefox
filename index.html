FAQ

  • What’s the privacy situation?

    We're using a utm_source parameter to count how many downloads come from these badges. The images are hosted on a Mozilla CDN for convenience and performance only. We do no tracking of traffic to the CDN. You can learn more about how Mozilla handles data in our privacy policy.

  • Can I use my own image as a badge?

    It’s your site, go for it! We just ask that you don’t trick people into going to the download page without them knowing what's going on.
